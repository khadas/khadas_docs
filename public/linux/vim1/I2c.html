I2C

This guide is about how to access i2c on Ubuntu.

Connections

  • VIM1
  • VIM2
  • VIM3/VIM3L

I2C A
Sensor SCL <-> I2C_SCK_A (PIN 22)
Sensor SDA <-> I2C_SDA_A (PIN 23)
Sensor GND <-> GND
Sensor VCC <-> 5V or 3.3V //depends on your sensor
I2C B
Sensor SCL <-> I2C_SCK_B (PIN 25)
Sensor SDA <-> I2C_SDA_B (PIN 26)
Sensor GND <-> GND
Sensor VCC <-> 5V or 3.3V //depends on your sensor
